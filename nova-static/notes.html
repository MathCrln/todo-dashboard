Thursday, March 13th, 2019

The beginning of a nightmare

Yesterday, I went to a café in Hong Kong, and I met this wonderful, gorgeous girl. She was painting what seemed like a very good interpretation of The Hunchback of Notre Dame. As I leaned toward her, I saw it. She was some kind of monster. Maybe she was even the very monster she was painting…
